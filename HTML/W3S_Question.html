  2. HTML Paragraphs

    • hr tag : 수평선을 만들어 준다.

      • London

      London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

    • pre tag : 띄어쓰기한 문장을 전부 띄어쓰기 처리 해준다.
      • 
  My Bonnie lies over the ocean.

  My Bonnie lies over the sea.

  My Bonnie lies over the ocean.

  Oh, bring back my Bonnie to me.

  3. HTML Formatting

    • strong tag : 글자를 굵게 만든다.

      • WWF's mission is to stop the degradation of our planet's natural environment.

    • em tag : 글자를 기울게 한다. i tag도 가능

      • Tokyo

      Tokyo is the capital of Japan, the most populous metropolitan area in the world.

    • mark tag : 글자에 highlight를 준다.

      • HTML is FUN to learn!

    • sub tag : 아래첨자가 달리게 한다.

      • H 2 O is the scientific term for water.

    • del tag : 글자에 지움 표시를 해줌

      • My favorite color is blue red.

  4. HTML Quotations
    • q tag : 글자에 "..."표시를 통해 인용문임을 알린다.

      • I am so cool .

    • blockquote tag : 인용사이트를 안내해준다.
      • For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.
    • bdo tag : 문장의 출력 방향을 정해준다. rtl(right-to-left),ltr(left-to-right)
      • What a beautiful day! What a beautiful day!
    • abbr tag : 약어임을 알리는 표시

      • The WHO was founded in 1948.
